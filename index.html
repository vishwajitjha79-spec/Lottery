How to Play
Everything you need to know
Reading the Story
Scroll down to read. Each scene element fades in as you go. There is no wrong pace. Take your time.
Making Choices
At key moments, choice buttons will appear. Your decisions affect how the story unfolds and what information Felicia uncovers. Some choices lock or unlock paths permanently.
The Phone
From Chapter 1 onward, you will have access to Felicia's phone. Check messages, files, and calls for clues. Some content only appears after you reach certain points in the story.
Saving
The game auto-saves at key story moments. You can also export your save as a file and import it on any device. Go to Settings to manage your save data.
Install as App
On mobile, tap Share then Add to Home Screen to install TRACES as an app. It works offline once installed.
